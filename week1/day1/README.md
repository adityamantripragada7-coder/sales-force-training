## 💡 What is CRM?
**CRM (Customer Relationship Management)** is a system used by companies to manage:
- customer details
- sales activities
- support interactions
- communication

---

## ☁️ What is Salesforce?
Salesforce is a **cloud-based CRM platform** used to manage:
- customers
- sales
- services
- business processes

---

## 🚀 Why Companies Use Salesforce
Companies use Salesforce to:
- manage customer data
- track sales
- automate tasks
- improve communication
- understand customers better

---

## 🧩 Salesforce Concepts

### 🏢 Account
Represents a **company or organization**

**Examples:**
- Amazon
- Infosys
- TCS

---

### 👤 Contact
Represents a **person related to an account**

**Examples:**
- customer
- employee
- student

---

### 💰 Opportunity
Represents a **possible business deal or sale**

---

## 🔄 Business Flow


### 📖 Explanation
- **Lead** → Interested person  
- **Contact** → Confirmed person  
- **Opportunity** → Possible deal  
- **Customer** → Final customer  

---

## 🌍 Real World Mapping (College Admission System)

- **Account** → College  
- **Contact** → Student  
- **Lead** → Student applying for admission  
- **Opportunity** → Possible admission  

---

## ⚖️ Admin vs Developer

### 🛠️ Admin
- manages setup  
- creates reports and flows  
- works without coding  

### 💻 Developer
- writes Apex code  
- builds applications  
- adds custom functionality
